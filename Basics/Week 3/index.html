Did you know?

  • Rats are very social animals.
    Rats love to hang out with eachother. They're extremely social animals and they can get depressed without friends. That's why you should always get rats in groups.
  • They're very clean!
    Rats love to groom themselves. They do so, multiple times a day. It's even part of their social behaviour, as the more dominant ones groom the rats that are submissive to them.
  • A group is called a mischief!
    As this word means playful misbehaviour, or trouble caused by someone or something, you might wonder why we'd call these cute little friends a mischief. Well, if you've ever met a rat, you'll know. Rats, like cats, ferrets, dogs and other pets, love to chew on things. Some rats are known to steal small items during free-roam time and stash them away in a nest.
  • These rodents play fair!
    Peace, love and understanding in the mischief can only come to fruition with a little play-fighting. Rats dont just decide who's the boss with these wrestling matches, but they also just kind of like it!
